# Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram) — Implementation Guide

## When to Recommend
E-commerce/DTC as the primary acquisition channel. Event promotion for restaurants. Awareness campaigns for fitness studios with $2K+ budgets. Not recommended for home services, professional services, or any business where the customer searches rather than discovers.

## Setup Checklist (Week 1)
1. **Install the Meta Pixel on the website.** This is non-negotiable before spending a dollar. The pixel tracks website visitors, add-to-carts, and purchases. Without it, Meta optimizes for clicks (worthless) instead of purchases.
2. **Set up Conversions API (CAPI).** Server-side tracking that catches what the pixel misses (Safari/iOS privacy blocks ~30% of events). Shopify and WooCommerce have one-click CAPI integrations.
3. **Create a product catalog** (for e-commerce). Connect the Shopify/WooCommerce product feed to Meta Commerce Manager. This enables dynamic product ads — the ads that show people the exact product they viewed on your site.
4. **Start with one campaign, one ad set, broad targeting.** Don't narrow the audience on day one. Meta's algorithm finds buyers better than manual targeting at budgets under $10K/month. Set the optimization event to "Purchase" (not "Add to Cart" or "Link Click").
5. **Launch with 3-5 creative variants.** Video (15-30 seconds, product in use, UGC-style) outperforms static images by 2-3x. Test different hooks in the first 3 seconds — that's where scroll-stop happens. Include at least one testimonial/review creative.

## Campaign Structure for SMBs
- **One campaign, Advantage+ Shopping** (for e-commerce). Let Meta's algorithm handle placement and audience. Manual campaign structures are for $20K+/month budgets.
- **Budget: $50/day minimum** for the algorithm to optimize. Below this, Meta doesn't get enough conversion data to find your audience. $100/day ($3K/month) is where performance typically stabilizes.
- **Creative testing cadence:** Add 2-3 new creative variants per week. Pause any ad with a frequency above 3.0 and a declining CTR. The creative is the targeting — when performance drops, it's usually creative fatigue, not audience exhaustion.

## What Good Looks Like
- **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend):** 3x+ for DTC at scale. A $30 product needs $10 or less in ad spend per purchase. New brands should target 2x ROAS while building data, then optimize toward 3-4x.
- **Cost per purchase:** Varies by AOV (average order value). Under $30 AOV: target $10-15 CPA. $50-100 AOV: target $20-35 CPA. Above $100 AOV: target $30-50 CPA.
- **Click-through rate:** 1.5%+ is healthy. Below 1% means the creative isn't stopping scrolls.
- **Frequency under 2.5** in prospecting campaigns. Above that, you're showing the same ad to the same people too often.

## Common Mistakes the Plan Should Flag
- **Optimizing for link clicks or landing page views.** Meta will find the cheapest clickers — people who click everything and buy nothing. Always optimize for the purchase event (or lead event for services).
- **Too many ad sets splitting the budget.** Three ad sets at $15/day each means none have enough data to optimize. One ad set at $50/day learns faster than five at $10.
- **Polished brand ads before UGC.** At budgets under $5K/month, UGC (user-generated content — selfie-style, phone-shot, real person using the product) outperforms studio creative consistently. Test UGC first, invest in production after finding winning angles.
- **Turning off ads too early.** Meta's learning phase needs ~50 conversions per week per ad set. Don't judge performance until you're past the learning phase (usually 5-7 days). Pausing and restarting resets the learning.
- **Boosting posts from the Instagram app.** This creates an engagement campaign, not a conversion campaign. Engagement doesn't equal sales. Always run ads through Ads Manager with a purchase or lead objective.

## Budget Guidance
- **$1,500/mo ($50/day):** Minimum viable. One campaign, broad targeting, 3-5 creatives. Enough to learn if Meta works for this product.
- **$3,000/mo ($100/day):** Performance stabilizes. Enough data for the algorithm. Start scaling winning creatives by 20% every 3 days.
- **$5,000+/mo:** Add retargeting campaign (10-15% of budget), test new audiences, and invest in creative production pipeline.
